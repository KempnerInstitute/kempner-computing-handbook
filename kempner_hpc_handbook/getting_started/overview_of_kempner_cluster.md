# Overview of Kempner Institute HPC Cluster

The Kempner institute has dedicated compute resources for its mission toward advanced research on intelligence from biological, cognitive, and computational perspectives. The following table provides an overview of the Kempner Institute HPC cluster:

| Specification             | A100 40GB                                      | H100 80GB                                             |
|---------------------------|------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| **Total GPUs**            | 144                                            | 96 (available), 384 (after adding 4 more racks)       |
| **Servers (per rack)**    | 36                                             | 24                                                    |
| **GPUs per Server**       | 4                                              | 4                                                     |
| **CPU Cores per Server**  | 64                                             | 96                                                    |
| **RAM per Server**        | 1 TB                                           | 1.5 TB                                                |
