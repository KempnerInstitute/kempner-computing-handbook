# KempnerPulse

[KempnerPulse](https://github.com/KempnerInstitute/kempnerpulse) is a terminal dashboard for NVIDIA DCGM hardware-counter metrics on AI clusters. It helps distinguish headline GPU utilization from actual SM, tensor-core, memory, and graphics-engine activity, with awareness of SLURM and CUDA GPU visibility.

**Best for**

- Monitoring live GPU jobs on SLURM-managed NVIDIA clusters.
- Inspecting per-GPU activity through Fleet, Focus, Plot, and Job views.
- Capturing or replaying DCGM metrics for debugging, comparison, and utilization analysis.
